Who is Stephen Tsoi-A-Sue?

Hi! I'm Stephen Tsoi-A-Sue an aspiring data professional based out of San Francisco.

My background is in finance and I've spent most of my professional career working as a Stock Plan Analyst contributing to the success of equity compensation plans at various companies.

I am genuinely excited about the growing field of data analytics, specifically the tools used in the process of gathering, cleaning, and analyzing data.

My curiosity compelled me to complete a course where I was exposed to advanced tools used in the process of data gathering, manipulation, analysis, and visualization.

This website is a compilation of the projects I enjoyed working on in my pursuit to hone my analytical skills.
